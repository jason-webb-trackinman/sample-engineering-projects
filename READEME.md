# Sample Engineering Projects

> The following is a small selection of engineering and systems integration projects completed during my career. These examples illustrate the types of technical problems I have solved across satellite communications, embedded systems, radio communications, tracking systems and large-scale systems integration.

> Client names have been generalised where appropriate to respect confidentiality.


### United Nations

**Objective**

Provide a personnel safety and tracking solution for peacekeepers supporting election activities in the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

**Solution**

Designed a satellite-based GPS tracking system for monitoring peacekeeper movements during election operations. The solution integrated portable GPS hardware, satellite communications and monitoring software to improve personnel awareness and operational safety while escorting election staff between remote villages.


### Private Client

**Objective**

Design and integrate all onboard technology systems for a luxury super-yacht.

**Solution**

Designed and delivered a complete systems integration solution covering navigation, communications, entertainment and vessel infrastructure. Responsibilities included system architecture, equipment specification, technical documentation, procurement, installation and commissioning.


### Multiple Satellite & Radio Communications Clients

**Objective**

Provide reliable file transfer over slow and intermittent satellite and radio communications links.

**Solution**

Designed and implemented a reliable store-and-forward communications protocol using an ACK/NAK retransmission process for low-bandwidth, high-latency and intermittent links. The protocol supported satellite, radio and SCADA communications where conventional networking methods were unsuitable.


### US Maritime Radio Coast Station

**Objective**

Automate digital radio equipment testing for maritime safety systems.

**Solution**

Developed base station software that received HF and VHF digital test calls, decoded incoming messages, logged results and automatically transmitted acknowledgements. The system supported equipment testing within the Global Maritime Distress and Safety System (GMDSS) and remained operational for more than ten years, including supporting maritime operations during the 2005 Hurricane Katrina response.


### Private Client

**Objective**

Monitor vehicle speeds approaching from multiple directions using a single stationary installation.

**Solution**

Designed and developed a Doppler radar measurement system capable of simultaneously detecting and displaying vehicle speeds from multiple directions, improving situational awareness from a fixed observation point.


### European Defence Client

**Objective**

Provide real-time positional awareness for helicopters during military training exercises.

**Solution**

Developed a standalone real-time tracking system that enabled participating helicopters to monitor each other's positions during training operations, improving coordination and operational awareness.
